num_simulations = 2000
sample_size     = 100

vec_xbar = [None] * num_simulations

for iteration in range(num_simulations):
    vec_unif  = np.random.uniform(low = -2, high=2, size = sample_size)
    vec_xbar[iteration] = vec_unif.mean()

vec_xbar

Answer: As sample size increases, the distribution of the sample means become more concentrated and precise, reduces variance. As number of simulations increase the histogram is more smooth and less irregular.
